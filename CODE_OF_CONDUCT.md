# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss technical issues in good faith.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to project maintainers through repository contact channels.
